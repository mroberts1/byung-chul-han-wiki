## Rules

- One tag per BCH book's central concept (e.g. `#shanzhai`, `#transparency`)
- Cross-cutting concepts get their own tag only if they recur across multiple books
- No generic tags: not `#philosophy`, `#chinese-thought`, `#ontology`
- Hyphenated, lowercase: `#burnout-society` not `#BurnoutSociety` or `#burnout_society`
